Galactic Warfare!

Up to 4 controllers/keyboards

Keyboard Controls:
Keyboard
Join: Spacebar
Throttle: W/S or Up/Down keys
Rotation: mouse pointer
Shoot: Left click
Missiles: Rightclick
Implosion bomb: Q
Bomb: E
Upgrade fire rate: R (30 crystals)


Gamepad Controls:
Join: Start button
Throttle: left joystick up and down
Rotation: Right stick
Shoot: Right Trigger
Missiles: Left trigger
Implosion bomb: Left bumper
Bomb: Right bumper
Upgrade fire rate: Button North (30 crystals)
